What is Remedial Massage

Remedial massage is a therapeutic form of massage therapy that focuses on assessing and treating musculoskeletal conditions and injuries. It involves a systematic approach to identifying areas of tension, dysfunction, or pain in the body and then applying various manual techniques to alleviate these issues.
